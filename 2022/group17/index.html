    Our Traffic Light System is an all-in-one platform for managing your projects-based modules. It addresses actual user pain points and will renovate the delivery of the class.

    Get StartedView on GitHub

    Why Traffic Light System

    Lecturers and TAs need an integrated platform to boost daily efficiency and keep track of students' progress. We have designed our system to precisely meet these needs.

    Efficient Workflow

    Lecturers and TAs can take advantage of our rich feature sets to boost their day-to-day work efficiency. From issue tracking board to one-click bulk import, the system will handle all the heavy lifting for the users.

    Streamlined Issue Tracking

    TAs can raise issues to lecturers using our tracking board to keep everything in check.

    Instant System Backup

    Admins can backup the entire system's data with one click. No more worries about data loss.

    Effortless Bulk Import

    Lecturers can conveniently import project data and students' pictures to quickly set up the system.

    Intuitive Visuals

    We understand keeping track of a vast volume of data from students's contribution to overall module performance can be overwhelming. We designed our system with easily digestible visuals in mind so that users can quickly grasp the information from the data.

    Team Performance at a Glance

    Our system will automatically generate team performance bar chart for the whole module or a specific team, using high-contrast traffic light colors.

    Interactive Student Data

    Lecturers and TAs can click on the bar chart column to view relevant student data in more detail. No more tedious data filtering.

    Track Performance Trend with Ease

    Our system will automatically generate a line chart tracking the performance trend for all students. No one will be left behind.
